Once upon a time.... A little elf lived in a village, this village was ravaged by evil moles out for blood,
These moles are not alone though. For they are united and led under the massive and demonic treant, with the 
formation of this hellish army the boss treant is looking to pillage and rule over all of the land. The elf 
is out for vengeance and determined to stop this overwhelming evil force that has awakened, The Elf looks to stop and
purge the land of its evil and wrong doings. Is this his end....... or is it just the beginning!
